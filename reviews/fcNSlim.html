Review: The Faber Castell Neo Slim

A sleek, painfully minimalist pen

November 1, 2015 N.N.

According to their website, Faber Castell released the Neo Slim to appeal to "young professionals and trendsetters" as they note on their website and the pen certainly has that Millenial-minimalist aesthetic. For my tastes, if it were any more simple it would bland like a room decorated in only beige bookshelfs and white "modern" vases. Thankfully, the Neo Slim rides that line. My version is shiny, almost too shiny, slim, almost too slim, and has two wonderfully rounded concave divits at its two ends.
